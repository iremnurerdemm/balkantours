MOSTAR BRIDGE

The Mostar Bridge is not just an engineering marvel; it is also a cultural symbol. Traditional activities such as jumping from the bridge into the Neretva River make it an extraordinary experience. However, the bridge suffered damage during the Bosnian War and was reconstructed in 2004 using original materials.
